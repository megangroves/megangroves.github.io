How I Work

Services

I typically work with companies and co-founders in one of 3 ways:

Advising

As your advisor, I support you in making critical decisions about growth, financing, relationship management, product development, and more, and I connect you to appropriate industry contacts. I am your advocate, your devil’s advocate, and your partner in the long-term health of your company. If you are building your Board, let's get to know each other.

Mentoring

As a mentor for both Highway1 and H-Farm Ventures, I meet with early stage companies on an individual basis to provide feedback and focus, or I give group talks on a sought-after topic. If you would like me to participate in your acceleration program, whether in-person or remotely, please get in touch.

Consulting

My consulting services can be strategic (I help you with essential planning and articulation of your strategy) or tactical (I help you create repeatable processes or serve as stand-in help to your company). Consulting can be just the touch of magic your company needs to transform. To set up a free 30 minute conversation to talk about your roadmap by Skype or in-person, please send me a note.

About Me

Megan Groves

Advisor. Mentor. Consultant.

Megan Groves is an early stage advisor, startup mentor, and business consultant.

After founding two of her own companies, one of which was acquired, she developed plans integral in the acquisitions of 4 other startups. She then decided to apply her experience to the corporate world where she worked closely with Fortune500 executives such as Microsoft, OpenText, AT&T, Sony, and Harley Davidson to create systems for growth and transformation.

Today Megan is based in Silicon Valley and heads up Analytics & Insights at Federated Media, a brand influencer marketing and ad-tech company, and continues her involvement in the startup community through mentoring and advising.

She has been a mentor at San Francisco’s hardware incubator Highway1 since its inception, and gives talks on branding and metrics at H-Farm, Italy’s top incubator-accelerator.

Megan has consulted with or advised over 125 startups and 50 enterprises around the globe. Her passion areas include food products and services businesses, content-heavy SaaS products, wearables, 3D printing, and growth in international markets.
